Quick steps to open a bank account

1

Choose the account type

Decide between a savings, current/checking, or fixed deposit account based on your needs.

2

Prepare required documents

Typically: government ID (passport/ID card/driving license), proof of address (utility bill/rental agreement), passport photo, and sometimes proof of income.

3

Fill the application

Complete the form (online or at a branch). Provide correct personal details and upload/attach documents.

4

Initial deposit & verification

Some banks require a minimum opening deposit. Bank will verify your documents and may conduct KYC checks.

5

Receive account details

Once approved, you'll get account number, debit card, and online banking access instructions.
